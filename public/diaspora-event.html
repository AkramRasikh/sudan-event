Diaspora: Voice of the revolution

This event was all about discussing in detail about what the role of the diaspora is in the revolution - particulary post the 3rd June massacre. Many of us have sat and thought what can we actually do. This event broke down our role into political lobbying, social media lobbying and creating a support network. A great campaign was also launched and that was the Fathers Day Campaign where children were encouraged to take an image with their fathers holding signs that say ‘My gift to you dad is ….’ followed by a statement that tackles an issue in Sudan. This campaign was effective in using the fathers day trend to highlight the plight of the Sudanese people.
